About Students' Council

Students' Council is the apex student body at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College Of Engineering. The primary duty of the council comprises of standing strong as a bridge of formal communication between the students, faculties and the administration. While it seeks to address any queries and resolve issues that the students or admin may face, the Council also relentlessly strives towards the enrichment of the campus experience. Our hearts all beat for a single purpose which is,
“To serve and protect the honour and integrity of the council, always.”
STUCO stands stronger!
